# Genesis AI

## Intelligent Knowledge and Decision Support System

Genesis AI is an intelligent knowledge organization and decision support platform designed to help people transform information into structured knowledge and better decisions.

Rather than replacing human thinking, Genesis AI assists users in organizing ideas, connecting concepts, documenting projects, and exploring possible solutions through artificial intelligence.

## Inspiration

Genesis AI was inspired by the belief that AI should strengthen human reasoning instead of replacing it. The project encourages people to organize knowledge, understand complex systems, and create solutions with humans always remaining at the center of the decision-making process.

## What it does

- Organizes knowledge
- Supports decision making
- Structures ideas into projects
- Connects information from different domains
- Assists learning and documentation
- Encourages systematic thinking

## Technologies

- GPT-5.6
- ChatGPT
- Markdown
- GitHub

## Future Vision

Genesis AI aims to evolve into a collaborative ecosystem where people can transform ideas into projects, systems, automations, and digital solutions with AI assistance.

## Built with GPT-5.6

GPT-5.6 was used throughout the project to assist with brainstorming, software architecture, documentation, content organization, and refinement of the project's concepts and presentation.
